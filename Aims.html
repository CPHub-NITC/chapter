Selection Process

These are our selection guidelines

No. Overview
1 One recruitment test at the beginning of every semester.
2 Announcement
3 First screening round - Online Quiz
4 First shortlist
5 Second screening round - Contest on Codechef
6 Final shortlist
No. Shortlist Criteria:
1 Candidates are divided based on the year of graduation.
1.1 Example 1: BTech 2017, BArch 2016, MTech 2019, MCA 2018 etc.
1.2 Example 2: BTech 2018, BArch 2017, MTech 2020, MCA 2019 etc.
2 From each year a maximum of 12 members (4 teams of 3 members each ) will be present at any time in the campus-chapter.
3 one team (consisting of 3 members ) from the non-CSE background from each year is selected for sure.
4 All the other 9 members out of 12 are exclusively selected based on their merit and the vacancy after the two screening rounds
5 Within an equivalent class priority of selection is based on rank in the respective contest only.
6 Candidates with more attempts left for ICPC are given more importance over the other candidates.
7 Codechef and chapter members have the authority to rule out a candidate if they sense that candidate has been involved in malpractice during one of the two contests.
No. What we consider
1 Candidate's logical ability.
2 Competitive Programming skills.
3 motivation to work for the club.
No. What we don't
1 Consider any priorities for selecting the rest 9 members out of 12 members from each year ( open to all the candidates from respective divisions regardless of branch of study ) .
No. Announcement:
1 Poster, Google Form, Email.
2 All the information regarding screening rounds will be notified to candidates before the events.
No. First shortlist:
1 At least twice the required number of candidates are shortlisted after the First screening round.
2 Email Notification and confirmation.
No. Second screening round - Contest on Codechef:
1 A Codechef contest will be conducted by the chapter members to judge a candidate's competitive programming skills.
No. Final shortlist:
1 Shortlist of the exact required number of Candidates after the second screening round. All the shortlisted candidates are monitored for 2 weeks wherein we check their interest in Competitive Programming and their participation in CodeChef, Codeforces, etc .also all the candidates must participate in weekly ICPC mirrors with their team.
2 Email Notification and confirmation.
2.1 A short interview to judge interest in CP may involve checking activity on coding platforms.
2.2 In case a candidate is not available or rejected then the next candidate in the rank list is given preference.
3 We repeat we don't consider anything other than logical skills( through online Quiz ) , CP skills ( through contest and interview ) , motivation to work for the club.
4 Final shortlist is announced after the 2-week monitoring program.
