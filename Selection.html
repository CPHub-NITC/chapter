Selection Process

Always be yourself and have faith in yourself. Do not go out and look for a successful personality and try to duplicate it.

Bruce Lee

These are our selection guidelines


Overview

  1. One recruitment test at the beginning of every semester
  2. First screening round - Online Quiz
  3. First shortlist
  4. Second screening round - Contest on Codechef
  5. Final shortlist

Shortlist Criteria

  • Candidates are divided based on the year of graduation.
    • Example 1: BTech 2017, BArch 2016, MTech 2019, MCA 2018 etc.
    • Example 2: BTech 2018, BArch 2017, MTech 2020, MCA 2019 etc.
  • From each year a maximum of 12 members (4 teams of 3 members each ) will be present at any time in the campus-chapter.
  • one team (consisting of 3 members ) from the non-CSE background from each year is selected for sure.
  • All the other 9 members out of 12 are exclusively selected based on their merit and the vacancy after the two screening rounds
  • Within an equivalent class priority of selection is based on rank in the respective contest only.
  • Candidates with more attempts left for ICPC are given more importance over the other candidates.
  • Codechef and chapter members have the authority to rule out a candidate if they sense that candidate has been involved in malpractice during one of the two contests.

What we consider

  • Candidate's logical ability
  • Competitive Programming skills
  • motivation to work for the club

we don't

    Consider any priorities for selecting the rest 9 members out of 12 members from each year ( open to all the candidates from respective divisions regardless of branch of study ) .

Announcement

  • Poster, Google Form, Email.
  • All the information regarding screening rounds will be notified to candidates before the events.

First screening round - Online Quiz

  • An online Quiz to judge the candidate's logical reasoning

First shortlist

  • At least twice the required number of candidates are shortlisted after the First screening round.
  • Email Notification and confirmation.

Second screening round - Contest on Codechef

  • A Codechef contest will be conducted by the chapter members to judge a candidate's competitive programming skills.

Final shortlist

  • Shortlist of the exact required number of Candidates after the second screening round. All the shortlisted candidates are monitored for 2 weeks wherein we check their interest in Competitive Programming and their participation in CodeChef, Codeforces, etc .also all the candidates must participate in weekly ICPC mirrors with their team.
  • Email Notification and confirmation.
    • A short interview to judge interest in CP may involve checking activity on coding platforms.
    • In case a candidate is not available or rejected then the next candidate in the rank list is given preference.
  • We repeat we don't consider anything other than logical skills( through online Quiz ) , CP skills ( through contest and interview ) , motivation to work for the club.
  • Final shortlist is announced after the 2-week monitoring program.
