About CP Hub

CP Hub is a programming club operated and maintained by CodeChef Campus Chapter representatives and in association with CodeChef.Aim of CP Hub is to foster Competitive Programming culture in NIT Calicut and help students to tackle challenges involved in Problem Solving through computer programming and in turn advance their career development.

Vision and Mission


"Groom NITCians to stand out from crowd and compete in ACM-ICPC World Finals."

Objectives


Witness daily improvement in each one of us by conducting programming contests at college or Inter-NIT level in association with CodeChef.Overcome our weakness by conducting ACM-ICPC mirrors and competing among ourselves. Organizing Competitive Programming Workshop for spreading awareness and knowledge about Competitive Programming. In addition to these we strongly believe in

"All work and no play makes Jack a dull boy"


to comply with it we even organize non-conventional workshops based on interest of club members as part of recreational activities.
