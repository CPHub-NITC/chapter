Who are we?

CP Hub is a programming club focused primarily in the domain of Competitive Coding . Aim of CP Hub is to foster Competitive Programming culture in NIT Calicut and help students to tackle challenges involved in Problem Solving through computer programming and in turn advance their career development.

Our Weekly Events

Mirror

We conduct a weekly mirror taken from previous ICPC contests with the aim of providing practice to our members for ICPC.

Beginner Contest

We conduct weekly beginner contests for people new to the world of competitive programmming, the aim of beginner contests is to provide a platform for budding competitive programmers.

Workshops

We conduct workshops on a spectrum of topics ranging from GSoC to some advanced concepts used in competitive programming.
